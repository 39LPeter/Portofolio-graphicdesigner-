Out-of-Home (OOH) & Large Format Advertising

High-impact billboard and bus stop advertisements designed for maximum visibility and brand recall among commuters and pedestrians.

Fanta: Dynamic Orange Refreshment Billboard

This OOH advertisement for Fanta Orange was designed to instantly convey an overwhelming sense of refreshment and effervescence. Utilizing vibrant color contrast and a dynamic composition, the three stacked cans appear to burst with a splash of liquid and fresh orange slices. The design creates a powerful visual gravity that makes the product jump off the billboard, ensuring maximum visibility and instant appetite appeal for pedestrians and commuters. It's a testament to crafting compelling commercial visuals that stop traffic.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma

Nescafé: Classic Coffee OOH Campaign

This billboard celebrates the rich heritage and inviting aroma of Nescafé Classic. The design uses warm, sunny lighting and a blurred background to create a cozy, inviting atmosphere that makes the coffee the undeniable focal point. By showcasing raw coffee beans, a spoonful of granules, and the finished product, it tells a complete story of quality from bean to cup. The overall visual balance emphasizes the product's tagline, encouraging consumers to "Slay Every Moment" with a high-quality coffee experience.

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop
